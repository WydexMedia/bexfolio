Introduction

Bexfolio (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides trading education, free workshops, and webinars. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

By using our website or submitting information to us, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.

Data We Collect

Information you provide

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, city)
  • Form responses for workshops/webinars or inquiries
  • Payment details processed by our payment partners (we do not store full card data)

Information we collect automatically

  • Device and usage data (IP address, browser, OS, pages viewed)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies)
  • Approximate location inferred from IP (where legally permitted)

Information from third parties

  • Advertising/analytics partners (e.g., campaign performance, referral source)
  • Payment processors (transaction status, limited metadata)

How We Use Data

  • Provide, operate, and improve our website, workshops, and webinars
  • Respond to your queries and send service communications
  • Register you for sessions and share access information
  • Process payments and provide receipts via processors
  • Run analytics, A/B tests, prevent fraud and abuse
  • Send marketing (with consent or as permitted by law) — you can opt out anytime
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Consent (e.g., marketing emails, certain cookies)
  • Contract (to provide requested services)
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., analytics, service improvement, security)
  • Legal obligation (e.g., tax/financial compliance)

Sharing & Disclosures

We share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our services, such as:

  • Hosting, CRM, email service, analytics and advertising partners
  • Payment processors for transactions
  • Professional advisors and authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Policy (e.g., service delivery, legal/accounting obligations). We then delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, update, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Data portability (receive a copy in a portable format)
  • Lodge a complaint with your data protection authority

To exercise rights, see Contact Us.

Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies for the site to function and optional cookies (analytics/ads) to understand usage and improve services. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

  • Essential security, session, consent storage
  • Analytics traffic and engagement metrics
  • Advertising measuring campaign performance

Marketing Communications

If you subscribe, we may send updates about workshops, webinars, and offers. You can unsubscribe via the link in any email or by contacting us.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries different from your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

Changes to This Policy
